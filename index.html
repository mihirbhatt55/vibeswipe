👗 What is VibeSwipe?

VibeSwipe is a next-gen peer-to-peer fashion marketplace where users buy, sell, or swap thrifted clothing through swipe-based matching. AI-generated avatars help visualize fit and style. It’s like Tinder meets Depop, but smarter and more sustainable.

  • 💃 AI-powered avatars to visualize how clothes fit your body
  • 🤝 Swipe-to-match with real people sharing your vibe & size
  • 📦 List, buy, auction, or trade fashion with ease
  • 🏪 Future: connect with local thrift shops + events
